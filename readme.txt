== Description ==
This plugin offers an easy way to integrate Pulpix video feeds on your site.

Pulpix delivers engaging video feed experiences from articles or videos. Our AI engine will match any article to the most relevant video in the content pool, and deliver them in a fast and seamless video feed experience, that you can monetize with high-quality video ads.

**Learn more at [pulpix.com](https://www.pulpix.com).**

== Installation ==

= Requirements =
* PHP 5.2 or higher
* WordPress v3.0.x or higher

= Installation Process =
* Log into the WordPress admin for your site
* Go to 'Plugins' -> 'Add New'
* Search for Pulpix. Once found click on 'Install Now'
* Select 'Activate Plugin' from the download page or go to 'Plugins' -> 'Installed Plugins' -> 'Activate Plugin'

= Getting Started =
* Select 'Settings' -> 'Pulpix', which will navigate to Pulpix's setting page.
* Enter your Website Id as defined in your Pulpix account. If you don't have one, you first have to sign-up here: [https://dashboard.pulpix.com/signup](https://dashboard.pulpix.com/signup)
* Click 'Save Changes'
* The Pulpix script now loading on your site. It will take up to 48h for the engine to analyze, process and index your content. You will then see the first video feeds appear on your site!
* to manage advanced settings, customizations and track stats, log-in to your Pulpix account: [https://dashboard.pulpix.com/login](https://dashboard.pulpix.com/login)
